**Leave Policy**

1. **Annual Leave**: Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year. Unused leave can be carried over up to a maximum of 5 days.
2. **Sick Leave**: Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave annually. A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.
3. **Parental Leave**: New parents are eligible for 12 weeks of paid parental leave.
4. **Unpaid Leave**: Requests for unpaid leave must be approved by the department manager and HR.
