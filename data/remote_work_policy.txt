**Remote Work Policy**

1. **Eligibility**: Employees with at least 6 months of tenure are eligible to apply for remote work.
2. **Schedule**: Remote work is allowed for up to 3 days per week (Hybrid model). Wednesdays are mandatory in-office days for all teams.
3. **Workspace**: Remote employees must have a dedicated, quiet workspace with a stable internet connection.
4. **Availability**: Remote employees must be available during core business hours (10:00 AM to 3:00 PM) and respond to communications within a reasonable time.
