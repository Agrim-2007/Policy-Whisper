**Human Resources Policy**

1. **Employment Basics**: All employees are hired on an at-will basis. The standard work week is 40 hours, Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
2. **Compensation**: Salaries are paid bi-weekly on Fridays. Performance reviews are conducted annually in December.
3. **Benefits**: Full-time employees are eligible for health, dental, and vision insurance starting on the first day of the month following their hire date.
4. **Termination**: Employees wishing to resign are requested to provide at least two weeks' notice.
