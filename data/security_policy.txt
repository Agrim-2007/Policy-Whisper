**Information Security Policy**

1. **Passwords**: Passwords must be at least 12 characters long, include special characters, and be changed every 90 days.
2. **Device Security**: All company laptops must have full-disk encryption enabled. Unattended devices must be locked.
3. **Access Control**: Access to sensitive data is granted on a need-to-know basis. Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all internal systems.
4. **Incident Reporting**: Any suspected security breach must be reported to the IT Security team immediately via the emergency hotline.
