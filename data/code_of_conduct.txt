**Code of Conduct**

1. **Professionalism**: Employees are expected to treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect and dignity. Harassment or discrimination of any kind is strictly prohibited.
2. **Conflict of Interest**: Employees must avoid situations where personal interests conflict with company duties. Outside employment must be disclosed.
3. **Company Assets**: Company equipment and resources are for business use only. Limited personal use is permitted if it does not interfere with work.
4. **Social Media**: Employees should not post confidential company information on social media.
